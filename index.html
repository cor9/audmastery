What Is Audition Mastery?

A high-impact, 12-week audition program designed to strengthen on-camera instincts, elevate self-tapes, clarify choices, and help young actors begin booking consistently. The curriculum is based on Corey Ralston's signature training approach and includes weekly directed lessons, hands-on exercises, and a final mock audition.

What Your Actor Will Learn

A comprehensive 12-week journey through audition mastery, from understanding the casting process to delivering memorable callbacks.

Week 1

Understand Casting Process & Sides Breakdown

Review of casting process, understanding role sizes, how to breakdown sides, rehearsal strategy, and Prep101 fundamentals.

Week 2

Subtext & Non-Verbal Communication

Importance of subtext, elevating the script, use of non-verbal communication as subtext and human behavior/layers.

Week 3

Character & Emotional Layering

Character development, use of empathy, understanding emotional layers and complex emotion utilization.

Week 4

Comedy Blueprint

Types of humor, comedy rules, commitment to character, getting the laugh/landing the joke, sitcom rhythm and rules, single cam/multicam mastery.

Week 5

Drama Blueprint

Grounded realism, action, internal life, Uta Hagen method and techniques.

Week 6

Connection & Chemistry

Meisner method for connection and action plans, how to develop chemistry, partner scenework.

Week 7

Tone, Types & Tropes

TV/Film genres and tone, character types, using tropes as choices.

Week 8

Strong & Memorable Choices

Corey's program and philosophy on making strong choices, always seeking to own the character POV and make sure you are memorable.

Week 9

Charisma Super Power

Condensed charisma - book the room.

Week 10

Self Tape Standards

Tech, reader & success tips. Final scene prep and self tape instruction.

Week 11

Notes, Strategy & Redirects

Making the best self tape with coaching, feedback and strategy - perfect in-person preparation.

Week 12

Mock Callback Final

Individual mock callback "in the room" appointments. Follow guidelines for perfect meeting tips. Using charisma and keeping departments ready. Closing pep talk on their strengths, realities of industry.

About Corey Ralston

Acting coach, talent manager, advocate for young performers

Corey Ralston has spent over two decades guiding young actors in the TV & Film industry. Corey, himself, was a child actor since the age of seven in L.A.

He then went on to pursue talent management in 2017, where he currently represents kids and young adults at Bohemia Group. Corey's mission is to help as many young actors achieve success as he possibly can.

He founded the resource community Child Actor 101 for parents of youth actors, moderating discussions with a team of Industry Professionals, giving out frank free advice.

His coaching blends professional insight from talent management with practical, kid-friendly techniques that help actors grow quickly and authentically.

Parents and casting directors alike recognize Corey's coaching for its mix of strategic challenge and safe creative space. Breakthroughs aren't rare—they're expected. Many students have moved from uncertainty to callbacks, bookings, and real confidence under Corey's guidance.
